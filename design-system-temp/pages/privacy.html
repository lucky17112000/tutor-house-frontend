Privacy policy.

We collect the minimum we need to run the service, store it carefully, and never sell it. The full version follows — written in plain English, with a table you can actually read.

01The short version

We collect what we need to match you with tutors and run sessions — your account info, the sessions you book, and basic product analytics. We don't sell your data, we don't use third-party ad cookies, and we don't share your details with marketers. You can export, correct, or delete your data any time.

If you're in a hurry: we keep what we need to run the service, secure it carefully, and let you take it back whenever you ask. The rest of this page is the detail.

02What we collect

CategoryExamplesWhy
AccountName, email, password hash, photoTo run your account

03How we use it

Specifically, to:

  • Run the matching engine and surface tutors that fit you;
  • Schedule, host, and (if you opt in) record sessions;
  • Process payments and prevent fraud;
  • Send transactional emails — receipts, reminders, dispute updates;
  • Improve the product based on aggregated, de-identified usage data;
  • Comply with the law, respond to lawful requests, and protect rights and safety.

04Who we share with

We share data only with vetted processors who help us run the service:

  • Stripe — payments;
  • Daily.co — video sessions;
  • AWS — hosting, in encrypted form;
  • Postmark — transactional email;
  • Plausible — privacy-respecting product analytics (no cookies, no cross-site tracking).

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We do not share it with advertisers. If we're ever required to disclose data to law enforcement, we'll push back where lawful and notify you unless legally prohibited.

05How long we keep it

  • Account data: while your account is active, plus 90 days after deletion (for fraud and chargebacks).
  • Session recordings: 90 days by default; you can extend or delete any time.
  • Payment records: 7 years (US tax requirement).
  • Backups: rolling 30-day window, encrypted, then purged.

06Your rights

Wherever you live, you can:

  • Get a copy of your data — it exports as JSON from Settings → Data;
  • Correct anything inaccurate;
  • Delete your account and data (subject to the legal retention windows above);
  • Object to or restrict certain processing;
  • Withdraw consent for optional things (recordings, the newsletter) any time.

If you're in the EU/UK, this includes GDPR rights. If you're in California, this includes CCPA/CPRA rights. To exercise any of them, email privacy@tutorhouse.com — we respond within 30 days.

07Security

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Access is role-restricted; only a handful of senior engineers can read production data, and every read is audited. We run third-party penetration tests twice a year and publish a yearly security report.

If we ever discover a breach affecting your data, we'll tell you within 72 hours of confirming it.

08Kids & teens

You must be 13 or older to use Tutor House. For users 13–17, a parent or guardian must agree to the terms and may at any time review or delete their teen's data by emailing privacy@tutorhouse.com. We don't run targeted ads to minors. We don't sell minors' data, period.

09International

Tutor House is operated from the United States. If you're outside the US, your data is transferred to and processed in the US under standard contractual clauses. We don't transfer data to countries without an adequate level of protection unless you've explicitly agreed.

10Contact us

Privacy questions, data requests, or to lodge a complaint: privacy@tutorhouse.com.

EU/UK representative: tutorhouse.eu (at) gdprlocal.com. Mailing address: Tutor House Inc., 220 Atlantic Ave, Suite 300, Brooklyn NY 11201.
